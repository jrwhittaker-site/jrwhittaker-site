About

Still asking questions.

I came to filmmaking the way most do: by watching too many films and asking too many questions. Twenty years later the questions have not stopped. I have directed for Sony Pictures, the BBC, Gillette, Hyundai, and Nissan. I have produced for Netflix and Amazon. I have taught and mentored filmmakers across five continents. And I am still making short films and writing screenplays in Brooklyn because the work keeps teaching me things I do not yet know. Based in New York. Available worldwide.
